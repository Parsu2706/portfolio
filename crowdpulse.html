CrowdPulse: Cross-Platform Narrative Intelligence


üì∞ Description

CrowdPulse is an end-to-end cross-platform analytics system that analyzes discussions from Reddit and global news sources to detect trends, sentiment patterns, and emerging narratives. The system automatically collects data, performs preprocessing, sentiment classification, topic modeling, entity extraction, and cross-platform comparison. Results are presented through an interactive Streamlit dashboard, enabling real-time exploration of public opinion and media coverage.
